Internships

May-July, 2017

Effects of dispersion forces between nanogels and ions of Hofmeister Series

Dr. Alberto-Martin Molina, University of Granada, Spain

Used Monte Carlo simulation methodologies in C++ to analyze the effect of dispersion forces on properties like radius of gyration, electrostatic potential etc. by ions of the Hofmeister series on the widely studied charged nanogel poly-NIPAM at various temperatures. We are writing a manuscript to publish the results in a scientific journal.

May-July, 2016

Flow of Inelastic particles over a bumply inclined plane

Dr. Anurag Tripathi, Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur

Discrete Element techniques were used in C to model the flow of granular materials down a rough incline and studied properties like quality of collisions, liquid and solid-like behavior. New empirical correlations were formulated to relate viscosity and volume fractions with the inertial number.

Research Experience

Theoretical simulations of drug-eluting nnaogels (Undergraduate Thesis)

Dr. Prateek Kumar Jha, Indian Institute of Technology, Roorkee, India

Dr. Prateek Kumar Jha, Indian Institute of Technology, Roorkee, India
